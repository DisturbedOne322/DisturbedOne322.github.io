I love games! I`ve been playing computer games since childhood, and see myself playing them later on. I see games as a good way of entertaining yourself, there are so much of them that every living person will find on to their liking. But games can also be a work of art. When the developers put their soul into creation, make great music, gameplay, story, atmosphere, that`s when a game could be considered masterpiece, even if its indie developers. That`s the kind of games I like the most.
